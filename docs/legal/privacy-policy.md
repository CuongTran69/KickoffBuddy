# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2026-05-29**

## Introduction

Kickoff Buddy ("we", "our", or "the app") is a football companion app that helps you track World Cup match schedules, set reminders, and learn football rules and vocabulary. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

## What Data We Collect

We collect limited, non-personal analytics data to understand how the app is used and to improve the experience. Specifically, we collect:

- **App events**: actions such as completing onboarding, viewing a match, adding a match to your list, setting a reminder, planning a replay, viewing a rule card, searching vocabulary, and changing the app language.
- **Event metadata**: non-identifying attributes attached to events, such as the length of a search query (not the query text itself), whether a search returned results, and the topic/level of a rule card viewed.

We do **not** collect:
- Your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information (PII).
- Your location.
- The content of your search queries.
- Any financial information.

All analytics data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you individually.

## How We Use It

We use analytics data solely to:
- Understand which features are most useful.
- Identify and fix bugs or usability issues.
- Prioritize future improvements.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond improving the app.

## Third Parties

We use **Firebase Analytics** (provided by Google LLC) to collect and process analytics events. Firebase Analytics may transfer data to Google's servers in the United States or other countries. Google's privacy practices are described at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

## Data Retention

Firebase Analytics retains event data for up to 14 months by default. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us (see Contact section below).

## User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Deletion**: Request that we delete your data.
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics collection by turning off "Share Usage Data" in your device settings, or by disabling analytics in the app settings (if available).

**GDPR (EU/EEA users)**: You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and port your data. You also have the right to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

**CCPA (California users)**: You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete it, and to opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.

## Children's Privacy

Kickoff Buddy is rated 3+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
