## Justification for 3+ Rating

Kickoff Buddy is a utility app designed to help football fans track World Cup match schedules, set reminders, and learn about football rules and vocabulary. The content is entirely educational and informational in nature. There is no violence, gambling, adult content, or any other material that would be inappropriate for any age group. The app is suitable for the broadest possible audience, including young children, and warrants the lowest possible age rating of 3+.
