## Tóm tắt nhanh

**Flutter app** companion cho FIFA World Cup 2026, nhắm người dùng Việt Nam (bilingual vi/en). ~14,700 LOC, 80 Dart files, ~75% hoàn thành.

### Kiến trúc
- **Pattern**: Feature-sliced (data / application / presentation) trong mỗi feature
- **State**: Riverpod (Provider, FutureProvider, StateNotifierProvider, NotifierProvider.family)
- **Routing**: GoRouter với shell route cho 5 tab chính
- **Storage**: Isar 3.x (Match collection) + SharedPreferences (flags)
- **API**: `worldcupjson.net` cho live scores + standings

### 8 Features
Home, Matches, Standings, Reminders, Replay Planner, Rule Cards, Vocabulary, Onboarding/Settings

### 3 Launch Blockers (phải fix trước khi submit store)

2. **Disclaimer "unofficial"** trong Settings/About — yêu cầu compliance
3. **UX dialog xin exact-alarm permission (Android 12+)** — chưa có
